# SnapHunt

<img width="333" height="222" alt="medium" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5f17963f-bbee-40a2-a927-e8375d942751" />

SnapHunt turns waiting into a real-world game. Instead of scrolling, explore your environment by finding objects to unlock fun facts and collectibles. Turn your idle moments into learning experiences!
